ABOUT THE GAME

Created by two brothers, Coffee Pot Terrarium is a turn-based game of tact and tile manipulation. Raise new pathways to traverse the map. Drop tiles to eliminate the enemies. Your skills charge each turn so you will need to tactfully decide when to activate them.

Each level introduces new challenges, enemies, and encounters to overcome. It's part tactical RPG, part puzzle game, with a result that is uniquely its own. Every feature of Coffee Pot Terrarium is being carefully designed, from the art and music to the code and gameplay. It's a true indie game, so if you like it, tell your friends about it!
